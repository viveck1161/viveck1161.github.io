    One Year experience in JAVA domain as a junior developer for the project National Insurance Company (NIC) India in HCL Technologies. NIC – EASI (National Insurance Company Enterprise Application Solution for Insurance) is an enterprise solution built for NIC. This solution is an integration of Enterprise applications such as EBao (vendor product for insurance), Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Human Resource Management System (HRMS), Master Data Management (MDM), Oracle Applications, IDM, Documentum and Oracle Business Intelligence. The core Insurance part (EBao) is divided into two major streams, General System (GS) and the General Claim System (GCS).GS comprises of handling all the business such as New Business, Renewal and Endorsement. GCS comprises of handling all the Claims related to the policy. Integration track is responsible to integrate EBao product which is the core part of Insurance in the project, with all other systems like CRM, MDM, IDM, HRMS, and Documentum etc. Web services are used in much context to integrate with all the other systems. The existing eBao data model was customized to reach and derive the functionality required for NIC project. Policy Schedules and letters required for the insurance system was designed and generated using Oracle BI publisher (OBIEE).
